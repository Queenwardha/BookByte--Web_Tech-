Our Story

BookByte was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to share our passion for exceptional literature with the world. What started as a small bookstore downtown has grown into a community of book enthusiasts and a home for those who appreciate the art and joy of reading.

Our journey began when our founder, Emma Bennett, returned from traveling across the major literary hubs of the world. Inspired by the diverse stories and publishing traditions she encountered, Emma wanted to create a space where people could experience books in their purest form while learning about their origins and creation.

Today, BookByte stands as a testament to our unwavering commitment to quality, sustainability, and the joy of sharing great books with readers everywhere.

Our Values

Sustainability

We source our books through direct relationships with publishers who practice sustainable printing and use responsibly sourced paper products.

Quality

We meticulously select each title in our collection to ensure that every book meets our exacting standards of literary excellence.

Community

We believe books bring people together. Our spaces are designed to foster connection, conversation, and the shared joy of reading and discovery.

Education

We're committed to promoting literacy and learning through our book clubs, author events, and educational programs for readers of all ages.
